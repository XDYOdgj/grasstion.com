Sweet Hearts Girls' Thermal Underwear Set

Keep your little one warm and comfortable all winter with our 6-Piece Thermal Underwear Set. Designed for active girls, it combines soft waffle knit fabric with a stretch fit that moves with them, whether they're playing or exploring.

The set includes a long john top and bottom, making it easy to layer under jackets, sweaters, or other winter wear. The breathable waffle knit technology ensures warmth without the bulk, while the opaque design prevents see-through – perfect for all-day wear.

Handcrafted in the USA, this set is built for durability and quality. With sizes that run small, we recommend choosing one size up for the best fit. Ideal for every day and every adventure, this thermal set is a great addition to any child's wardrobe.

Key Features

  • Soft, breathable waffle knit material
  • Stretch fit for all-day comfort and movement
  • 6-Piece set – ideal for complete layering
  • Opaque design for a flattering fit
  • Made in the USA for quality and durability

Why Choose This Set?

Whether it's sledding down a hill or rushing to school, this thermal set keeps your child feeling warm and cozy in style. It's the perfect base layer for active play and cozy nights, combining practicality with playful design that girls will love.
