Carhartt Girls Hoodie - Cozy, Rugged, and Ready for Anything

This Carhartt Girls' Fleece Half-Zip Hoodie combines soft comfort with a bold, stylish design that's ideal for all-day wear. Perfect for school, outdoor play, or cozy downtime, it's built to keep your child warm without compromising on look or function. The lightweight fleece fabric offers breathability, while the rib-knit cuffs and hem add a tailored feel that stays secure. A full hood provides extra protection against the cold, and the included pouch pocket makes it practical for carrying small essentials like keys or snacks.
